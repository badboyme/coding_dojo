Status

The FreeBSD/PPC port is still a Tier 2 platform. That means it is being fully supported by our security officer, release and tool-chain maintainers.

Latest News

  • 03 April, 2009: Support for the PowerPC G% has been committed to SVN.
  • 03 March, 2008: Supported by Freescale® PowerQUICC III MPC8XX family system-on-chip development boards has been committed into CVS.
  • 27 February, 2008: FreeBSD 7.0 is the firs release to officially support the FreeBSD/PPC port.
